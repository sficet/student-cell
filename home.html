About SFI Student Notes Cell

The SFI Student Notes Cell is a student-led academic initiative under the Students' Federation of India (SFI), CET Unit. We aim to support and empower students by providing quality academic resources — made by students, for students.

What We Do

  • Curated syllabus-based notes verified by top-performing students.
  • Previous year question papers and answer keys for effective exam preparation.
  • Lab manuals, formula sheets, and cheat sheets for quick revision.
  • Peer-to-peer doubt clearing platform and community engagement.
  • Timely academic updates, announcements, and notifications.

Why It Matters

Academic pressure, time constraints, and inaccessible resources are real challenges. The SFI Student Cell ensures no student is left behind — fostering a culture of mutual support and collective success.

Our Team

The cell is coordinated by passionate SFI student members, mentors, and seniors across all departments. Together, we collect, verify, and organize academic materials to help every CETian succeed.

Connect With Us
